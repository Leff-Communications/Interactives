Benefits Plans and Programs: Vendor Contacts at a glance

Talent on Demand is your one-stop-shop for all things talent-related, and your starting point to learn about the numerous rewards, recognition and well-being plans and programs at Deloitte. If you still have specific questions after reviewing the benefits documentation via Talent on Demand, you can also call the Deloitte contact center at 1(800) DELOITTE for personalized assistance. However, some types of questions are best addressed by our benefits carriers and vendors directly.

Not sure what types of questions our vendors can help with? Here are some common questions they can address:

Medical

Is my current doctor in-network?

Is my medication covered under the plan?

What is my health insurance member ID number?

Medical Plans

The Deloitte medical plans are administered by Aetna, Anthem, and UnitedHealthcare (UHC). You can contact these vendors directly if you have specific questions pertaining to their coverage and benefits levels.
